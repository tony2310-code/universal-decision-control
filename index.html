Universal Decision Control System

Local-first. Stored on this device only. Export regularly. Storage: local

How to complete an entry fast

Step 1. Choose a decision pattern. Let it prefill your reframe and action rule.

Step 2. Write the trigger as facts only and write the automatic thought.

Step 3. Pick one emotion and rate intensity before.

Step 4. Adjust the reframe if needed, then pick one small action and a real deadline.

Step 5. After you act, fill in experiment result and evidence, then mark done.

Rule: if intensity before is 7 or higher, action and deadline are required.

Quick prompts
Use one prompt, then take one small action.
  • What is the fact, and what story am I adding?
  • What would I tell a friend who said this thought?
  • What is one small proof I can create in the next 2 hours?
  • What rule would I follow if I wanted less regret later?
  • What is the smallest next step that still counts?
