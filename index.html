Universal Decision Control System

Local-first. Stored on this device only. Export regularly. Storage: local

How to use this in 60 seconds

1. Write the trigger as facts only.

2. Write the first automatic thought.

3. Pick the emotion and rate intensity before.

4. Pick a distortion and write one calm reframe sentence.

5. Choose one action and a deadline. Then do it.

Rule: if intensity before is 7 or higher, action and deadline are required.

Quick prompts
Keep it short. Then pick an action and test the thought.
  • What is the fact, and what is the story I am adding?
  • If my best friend said this thought, what would I tell them?
  • What would a calm, disciplined version of me do next?
  • What is one small proof I can create in the next 2 hours?
  • What rule would I follow if I wanted less regret later?
