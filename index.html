Decode your behavior loop fast. Everything stays on your device unless you export.

1 to 3 words max
Keep every field short. Short inputs make patterns obvious.

Quick guide

Main rule
Every field must be 1 to 3 words max. Short answers make patterns easy to see.

How the sections work
1. Situation
Type what happened in 1–3 words.
Example: no replies

2. Thought
Use the dropdown list. These are common thoughts people have.
If your thought is not listed choose Other and type your own (1–3 words).
Example: nobody interested

3. Emotion
Choose the emotion you felt from the dropdown.
If it is not listed choose Other and type it (1–3 words).
Example: frustrated

4. Action
Choose the action you took from the dropdown.
Or choose Other and type it (1–3 words).
Example: stopped posting

5. Result
Write what happened after the action (1–3 words).
Example: no engagement

6. Correction
Write the better action you should take next time (1–3 words).
Example: post again

7. Save / Decode
Press Save to decode your behavior loop. The app will combine your thought, emotion, and action into a clear sentence so you understand what happened.

Insights section
This section shows patterns such as your most common thoughts, emotions, and actions. It helps you detect repeated behavior loops.

History section
Every entry you save appears here. You can review past behavior loops or delete entries.
